# Traits:
- **Constructed resistance**: +1 circuimstance bonus against posion & disase
- **Emotionally unaware**: -1 circuimstance penalty Diplomacy, Performance and Perception on sense motive
- **Living construct**: Dont need to eat or drink, Needs 2 hour of daily rest (still contious)
# Combat traits
- **Every ready**: Have reaction before 1. turn
- **Guardians armour**: Phy dmg res (1 + half lvl)
- **Intercept attack**: Reaction 10/15 feet ally gets physical dmg, I get the agro
- **Shield block**: Reaction
- **Taunt**: Makes an action against an ally **-1 circumstance penalty for attack rolls and DCs** and they become **off guard** till next round (120 ft #Long_distance_taunt)
- **Shielding Taunt**: **Raise a Shield**, and then **Taunt**. #auditory
